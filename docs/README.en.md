## What is Teanary?

Teanary is a **multi‑node, globally distributed e‑commerce backend** built with Laravel, optimized for:

* Cross‑border commerce
* Multi‑language & multi‑currency operations
* Region‑specific payment & deployment

**v1.4 extends this foundation with AI‑first operability.**

> Most e‑commerce systems help you *sell*.
> **Teanary helps you *operate*.**

---

## What’s New in 1.4 (Vision)

> Features listed below are **under design and development**.

### AI‑Native Operability

Teanary 1.4 treats AI as a **first‑class system participant**, not a plugin:

* Business insights (sales, traffic, conversion)
* Cross‑region performance analysis
* Operational suggestions (pricing, inventory risk)
* AI‑generated drafts (translation, SEO, campaigns)
* Anomaly detection & alerts

AI never accesses raw databases directly. All interactions are **scoped, auditable, and controlled**.

---

### MCP‑Ready Architecture

Teanary 1.4 is designed to support **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** style integrations:

* Explicit capability exposure
* Permission‑based access
* Deterministic execution
* Full audit logs

> AI can only do what Teanary explicitly allows.

---

## Design Principles

* AI‑assisted, not AI‑autonomous
* Human‑confirmable operations
* Explicit service boundaries
* No hidden automation
* Production safety first
