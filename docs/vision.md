# Teanary Vision

## AI‑Native Operable Commerce Infrastructure

---

## Why Teanary Exists

Modern e‑commerce systems are good at **transactions**, but weak at **operations**.

As businesses go global, complexity increases:

* Multiple regions
* Multiple languages
* Different payment & logistics rules
* Fragmented data

Teanary exists to solve **operability**, not just functionality.

---

## Core Belief

> **AI should assist operators, not replace them.**

Teanary does not believe in:

* Fully autonomous pricing bots
* Uncontrolled AI writing to production systems
* Black‑box decision making

Instead, we believe in:

* AI‑assisted decision making
* Explicit system boundaries
* Human‑confirmable actions

---

## What “AI‑Native” Means in Teanary

AI‑native does **not** mean:

* Chatbots everywhere
* Prompt‑driven business logic

AI‑native **means**:

* The system is designed to expose *meaningful operational context*
* AI can reason over structured, scoped data
* AI suggestions are explainable and traceable

---

## Capability‑First Architecture

Teanary exposes business abilities as **capabilities**, not raw data:

Examples:

* `getSalesSummary(period, region)`
* `analyzeConversionTrend(site)`
* `detectInventoryRisk()`

AI interacts only with these capabilities.

This ensures:

* Security
* Predictability
* Auditable behavior

---

## MCP Alignment

Teanary is designed to align with **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** concepts:

* Explicit capability declaration
* Read / Analyze / Suggest separation
* Strict permission scopes
* Full action logging

MCP is not treated as a trend, but as a **design discipline**.

---

## Multi‑Node as a First‑Class Concept

Global commerce is not one system deployed many times.

Each node in Teanary:

* Is a fully independent system
* Serves a specific market
* Shares only what should be shared

AI operates **across nodes**, providing global insight without centralizing risk.

---

## Long‑Term Direction

Teanary aims to become:

* A reference architecture for AI‑operable commerce
* A foundation for global, headless, intelligent storefronts
* A system where humans and AI collaborate safely

---

## What We Will Not Do

* We will not sacrifice safety for automation
* We will not hide complexity behind magic
* We will not optimize for hype over correctness

---

## Final Statement

> **Teanary is not building smarter machines.**
> **We are building better systems for humans to operate — with AI as a trusted assistant.**
